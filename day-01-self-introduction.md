## 二、今日词汇（30个高频口语词汇）

### 2.1 易忘高频动词（15个）

这些词你可能认识，但**不一定能主动使用**：

| 单词 | 中文 | 常见搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|---------|------|
| **accomplish** | 完成、实现 | accomplish a goal | I want to accomplish my goals this year. |
| **achieve** | 达到、实现 | achieve success | She achieved her dream of becoming a doctor. |
| **approach** | 接近、处理 | approach a problem | How should we approach this issue? |
| **attempt** | 尝试 | attempt to do | I'll attempt to finish it today. |
| **concentrate** | 集中注意力 | concentrate on | I can't concentrate with this noise. |
| **emphasize** | 强调 | emphasize the importance | He emphasized the need for teamwork. |
| **encourage** | 鼓励 | encourage sb to do | My boss encouraged me to take the course. |
| **establish** | 建立 | establish a relationship | We need to establish trust first. |
| **maintain** | 维持、保持 | maintain contact | It's hard to maintain a work-life balance. |
| **manage** | 管理、应付 | manage to do | I managed to finish the project on time. |
| **mention** | 提及 | mention that | He mentioned that he was leaving. |
| **participate** | 参与 | participate in | I participated in the meeting yesterday. |
| **persuade** | 说服 | persuade sb to do | I persuaded him to join our team. |
| **recommend** | 推荐 | recommend doing | I recommend starting early. |
| **remind** | 提醒 | remind sb to do | Please remind me to call her. |

**从"认识"到"会用"：**

| 方法 | 说明 |

## 三、口语部分：自我介绍

### 3.1 用高级词汇升级自我介绍

**对比一下：**

| 基础版 | 升级版 |
|--------|--------|
| I work as a PM. | I **currently work** as a product manager. |
| I want to improve my English. | I'm **determined** to improve my English. |
| I can do this job. | I'm **capable** of handling this role. |
| I like my job. | I'm **passionate** about my work. |
| I want to achieve my goals. | I'm **determined to accomplish** my goals this year. |

---

### 3.2 自我介绍模板（升级版）

**标准版（1分钟）：**
```
Hello, my name is [中文名], and you can call me [英文名].

I'm currently working as a [职位] at [公司].
I've been in this field for [年限] years, and I'm passionate about [领域].

I'm determined to improve my English because [原因].
In my free time, I enjoy [爱好] and I'm curious about [兴趣].

It's nice to meet you all!
```

**示例：**
> Hello, my name is Zhang Wei, and you can call me David.
>
> I'm currently working as a product manager at Alibaba. I've been in this field for 8 years, and I'm passionate about creating products that users love.
>
> I'm determined to improve my English because I want to communicate better with international colleagues. In my free time, I enjoy reading books about technology, and I'm curious about AI trends.
>
> It's nice to meet you all!
