## 三、口语部分：自我介绍

### 3.1 自我介绍5要素

| 要素 | 内容 | 时间 |
|------|------|------|
| **问候** | Hello/Hi | 2秒 |
| **姓名** | My name is... | 5秒 |
| **职业/身份** | I'm a... | 10秒 |
| **爱好/兴趣** | I like... | 15秒 |
| **结束语** | Nice to meet you | 3秒 |

---

### 3.2 核心句型

**问候：**
- Hello. / Hi.
- Good morning. / Good afternoon.

**姓名：**
- My name is [名字].
- I'm [名字].
- You can call me [昵称].

**职业：**
- I'm a [职业].
- I work as a [职业].
- I work at [公司].

**爱好：**
- I like [爱好].
- I enjoy [爱好].
- In my free time, I like to [活动].

**结束语：**
- Nice to meet you.
- It's nice to meet you all.

---

### 3.3 自我介绍模板

**简洁版（30秒）：**
```
Hi, I'm [名字].
I'm a [职业].
I like [爱好].
Nice to meet you!
```

**标准版（1分钟）：**
```
Hello, my name is [中文名], and you can call me [英文名].
I work at [公司] as a [职位].
I've been in this field for [年限] years.
In my free time, I enjoy [爱好1] and [爱好2].
It's nice to meet you all!
```
